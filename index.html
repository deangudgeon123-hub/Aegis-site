What Aegis does

Aegis provides independent governance and risk assessments for organisations using AI or complex data systems

  • AI governance and compliance gap assessments
  • Data handling and model risk reviews
  • Evidence based audit reports
  • Clear remediation guidance

How a pilot audit works

  1. Scoping call to understand systems and risk profile
  2. Assessment using limited redacted or synthetic data
  3. Aegis produces an audit and findings report
  4. Optional remediation guidance and follow up

What you receive

Every pilot includes

  • Written governance and risk report
  • Identified gaps and priority actions
  • Evidence suitable for internal review or external partners
  • Clear next steps

Security and boundaries

Aegis does not require production access

  • No live system access required
  • Data minimisation by default
  • Region locked processing
  • No long term data retention

Pricing

  • Pilot audits are offered on a fixed scope basis
  • Typical pilot fee £1,000 to £3,000
  • Fixed duration and deliverables
  • No long term commitment
