About Aegis

Aegis is the control system for AI in finance. We help banks and fintech innovators keep their automation safe, auditable, and accountable.

Built for Clarity

We combine oversight, analytics, and model transparency so every decision can be explained and every risk is spotted before it becomes a problem.

Core Values

Our principles keep intelligent finance transparent, fair, and resilient.

Integrity – Every decision should be traceable and fair.

Precision – We focus on clarity, not noise.

Protection – Our goal is to protect systems, data, and the trust of everyone who depends on them.

The Founder

Dean Gudgeon built Aegis after seeing how automation in finance can create invisible risks. His goal is simple: make AI in banking and fintech as transparent and accountable as the people who run it.
