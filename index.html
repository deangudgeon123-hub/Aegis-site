About Aegis

Aegis is the control system for AI in finance.

With new global regulations emerging from the EU AI Act to tightening oversight by the FCA and CFPB, financial institutions everywhere are being forced to make their algorithms transparent and accountable.

Aegis gives them that capability. It is a unified platform for oversight, analytics, and explainability that keeps every automated decision compliant by design.
